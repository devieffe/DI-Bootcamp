Avengers: Thor

Thor is one of the mightiest character, an outer world god, in the Marvel Cinematic Universe

Thor Odinson is a fictional character portrayed by Chris Hemsworth in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) media franchise, based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name and the Norse mythological god of thunder.

In the MCU, he is depicted as one of the most powerful Asgardians, Avengers, an ancient alien civilization with long ties to Earth, who humans consider to be gods. Thor wields a powerful hammer called Mjolnir, and is initially depicted as the arrogant heir to the throne of Asgard.
